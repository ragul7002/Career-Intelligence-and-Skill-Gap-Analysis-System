# AI-Powered Career Intelligence and Skill Gap Analysis System (CareerIQ AI)

## Problem Statement

Many students struggle to understand whether their current skills and resume meet the requirements of their desired job roles. Traditional ATS (Applicant Tracking System) checkers only provide a resume score and do not offer clear guidance on skill gaps, career readiness, or improvement strategies.

As a result, students often lack awareness of:

- The skills required for their target career.
- The skills they are currently missing.
- Their overall job readiness level.
- A structured learning path to improve their employability.

Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent system that can analyze a student's resume, compare it with industry job requirements, identify skill gaps, evaluate career readiness, and provide personalized recommendations to help students become industry-ready professionals.

---

## Proposed Solution

**CareerIQ AI** is an AI-powered Career Intelligence and Skill Gap Analysis System that analyzes resumes, calculates ATS compatibility, identifies missing skills, generates a Career Readiness Score, and creates personalized learning roadmaps to guide students toward their desired careers.

---

## Key Features

- Resume Upload and Analysis
- ATS Score Calculation
- Skill Extraction using NLP
- Skill Gap Detection
- Career Readiness Score
- Career Match Analysis
- Personalized Learning Roadmap
- Future Growth Prediction
- Interactive Analytics Dashboard

## Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to help students understand their current career readiness, identify missing skills required for their desired job roles, and provide a structured roadmap to improve their employability and become industry-ready professionals.

---

## Expected Outcome

The system will:

1. Analyze student resumes.
2. Compare skills with industry requirements.
3. Identify skill gaps.
4. Calculate career readiness scores.
5. Recommend personalized learning paths.
6. Predict future career growth after skill acquisition.

This enables students to make informed career decisions and systematically prepare for their dream jobs.
