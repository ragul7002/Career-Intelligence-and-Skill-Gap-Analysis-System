# Objectives of the Project

# AI-Powered Career Intelligence and Skill Gap Analysis System (CareerIQ AI)

## Main Objective

To develop an AI-powered platform that analyzes student resumes, identifies skill gaps for target job roles, evaluates career readiness, and provides personalized learning recommendations to improve employability.

---

## Specific Objectives

### 1. Resume Analysis
- To allow users to upload resumes in PDF format.
- To extract relevant information such as skills, education, certifications, and projects.

### 2. ATS Score Evaluation
- To calculate an ATS compatibility score based on job role requirements.
- To provide insights on resume effectiveness.

### 3. Skill Gap Identification
- To compare existing skills with industry-required skills.
- To identify missing skills needed for a selected career path.

### 4. Career Readiness Assessment
- To evaluate the user's readiness level for a target job role.
- To generate a Career Readiness Score based on skill matching and resume quality.

### 5. Personalized Learning Roadmap
- To recommend learning paths and skill development plans.
- To help users systematically improve their employability.

### 6. Career Match Analysis
- To match user profiles with multiple career opportunities.
- To identify the most suitable career options based on existing skills.

### 7. Future Growth Prediction
- To estimate future career readiness after acquiring recommended skills.
- To visualize expected improvement through interactive dashboards.

### 8. Progress Tracking
- To maintain user progress records.
- To track skill development and roadmap completion over time.

---

## Expected Outcome

The proposed system will help students:

- Understand their current career readiness.
- Identify missing industry-relevant skills.
- Improve resume quality.
- Follow a structured learning roadmap.
- Become industry-ready professionals.

---

## Conclusion

CareerIQ AI aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing intelligent career guidance, skill gap analysis, and personalized growth recommendations.
